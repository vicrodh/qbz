The Problem

Qobuz on Linux?
It's been complicated.

You pay for Hi-Res audio, but you've been stuck with browser limitations. Until now.

  • Web players cap output at 48kHz — goodbye, hi-res
  • No official desktop app for Linux users
  • Getting bit-perfect to your DAC? Nearly impossible
  • Audio resampling destroys the quality you paid for
Illustration: Browser limitations
The Solution

Pure Audio.
Zero Compromise.

QBZ bypasses every limitation. Native code, direct hardware access, bit-perfect output.

  • Full 24-bit/192kHz resolution — every sample, every detail
  • Direct DAC passthrough for true bit-perfect playback
  • Native FLAC decoding — no browser overhead
  • Gapless playback for seamless album experiences

Hi-Fi Playback Engine
